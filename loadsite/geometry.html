What is Geometry?

Geometry is a branch of mathematics that studies shapes, sizes, and the properties of space. It includes points, lines, angles, surfaces, and solids.

Types of Shapes

  • Circle: All points equidistant from the center.
  • Triangle: Three-sided polygon.
  • Square: Four-sided polygon with equal sides and angles.
  • Rectangle: Four-sided polygon with opposite sides equal.
  • Polygon: Closed shape with three or more straight sides.
